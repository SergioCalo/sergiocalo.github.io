About me

Welcome to my personal website!

My name is Sergio, and I am currently a PhD student at Pompeu Fabra University under the supervision of Anders Jonsson. My research interests include Reinforcement Learning, Machine Learning on graphs and Embodied AI. I'm graduate in Physics from the university of Santiago de Compostela, where I found my passion for AI. I am currently studying a master's degree in Intelligent Interactive Systems at Pompeu Fabra University, with the focus on machine learning (Computer Vision, Reinforcement Learning and Graph Neural Networks). My field of expertise is computer vision, with several projects carried out to date. In addition, I am collaborating as a neural network developer in a research project on computer cloud dynamics using deep learning and recurrent neural networks. In my personal time I'm also a nature lover and I'm really interested in biology, music and mathematics.
